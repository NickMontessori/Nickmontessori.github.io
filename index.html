Providence Teacher resource page

Hey everyone!
this is the work-in-progress project website for a child from Providence: A Christan Montessori Community!
this webpage is designed to meet the needs of the teachers of Providence but if you've stumbled upon this page then feel free to to read and learn about montessori
Montessori is a type of school that honors the child and what he or she needs to be succesful.
We at Providence use materials to teach children things like long division or color charts or even basic motor movements for younger children in an easier way for them to understand. This website has these works and many more that we use
click the works below for tutorials on how to use them courtesy of multiple montessori teachers.
